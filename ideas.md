# Additional ideas

## Fix: Starting player advantage and player order
The way the drafting works ensures the starting player an immediate bonus action which could be relevant.
Furthermore, player order could matter a lot.
If either or both turns out to be the case, then the following idea could help.

Generate X additional tableau tiles.
These are indicate turn order and are randomly handed to players to be placed on the players tableau before taking the first turn.

These tableau tiles will have no or negative effect for the starting player and positive effect for the latter players.
